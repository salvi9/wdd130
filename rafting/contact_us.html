FAQ

How does scheduling work?

To schedule with Rock N' Water, click on the "Make Reservation" button located on the home page. From there you can choose your preferred dates, preferred course, as well as a checklist of equipment we offer.

What equipment do we need to bring?

With our different types of bundles, we can provide you with the appropriate gear so that you can have a genuine white water rafting experience with us. You are more than welcome to deselct items in the checklist that you wont need us to provide.

How long of an event should we expect?

Our passes are good for the whole day. You are more than welcome to check in at any time and leave at anytime except during our blackout days in which we have our guest make timeslot reservation.
